Humble Origins

October 15 1958

The First recorded instance of a computer based video game is released out into the world, created by William Higinbotham the game was a virtual immitation of the sport tennis, with the player controlling a paddle while trying to hit a ball into the opponent's goal.

A business boom into arcades

1972

Released by Atari in 1972, Pong was released to become the first financial success of the video game industry. A simple repackaging and rebranding of tennis for two, the game took the concept and ran with it leading to incredible sales and the start of an industry

The Rise of Console Gaming

October 18, 1985

The release of Nintendo's "Nintendo Entertainment System"(or NES for short) brought about the boom of the video game industry from arcades into the home of consumers. This console which was originally marketted as more of a toy rather than a device for people to own was the reason why video games have grown to be as big as they are today.

Current Day

Present

Video games have grown as an industry exponentially since the old days with technology advancing, so have video games. a video game can reach graphical fidelity to the point where it may look like a movie, nearly indistinguishible from real life.

Fun fact

Video game music was originally made using a special chipset which would allow sounds to be generated through the system, muscicians had to have programming knowhow in order to write music for games!
